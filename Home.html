What We Do

At Vicious Cycles, we specialize in getting you the perfect bike for your needs. Not only do we have connections with many popular brands but we provide custom-built bikes. If you can’t decide on a bike or nothing seems right for you we are experts in building you the perfect custom bike. Our team has been dealing with building custom bikes for over a decade. We make sure to update you in person at every step of the build. Our goal is to get you your dream bike and never rush the process. If you're busy and can only come in once a week or once a month no problem. Your schedule is our schedule when it comes to your custom build.

Wanting a bike but not sure where to start no problem. There are many recourses we recommend to get you started on choosing your perfect type of bike. Here’s a website we recommend for getting started in the biking community Get Started. If you prefer to talk to us in person about choosing a bike we’ll be happy to have you. Our large team specializes in providing everyone with a bike that suits them so contact us, give us a call, or stop by during our open hours.

Have you ever had an experience where you just bought something? Well, here at Vicious Cycle we also provide free first-time repairs for customers and low-cost repairs after that. Don’t worry, we have a repair team that has dealt with many types of repairs. Still not sure about buying a bike? Here we provide a two-month return policy. Test out the bike for two months and if you don’t like it returns the bike for a full refund no questions asked.

We hope you visit us in the future. Good luck with your cycling adventures.
